Research Profile

Hi, I am Yayuan Li.

I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University (admitted in 2025), advised by Prof. Yinghuan Shi. My work focuses on knowledge transfer and training-free adaptation for large models, with a particular interest in model merging and modular capability composition.

Model Merging Knowledge Transfer Training-Free Adaptation Multimodal Learning

I enjoy turning "many small capable models" into "one reliable system".
我希望让模型协作像搭积木一样自然、可控、可复用。

Research Interests

  • Efficient reuse and composition of pretrained models.
  • Cross-modal transfer between language and vision representations.
  • Robust training-free methods for few-shot generalization.
